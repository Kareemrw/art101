Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Take out all the ingredients: butter, sugar, flour, salt, vanilla extract, and chocolate chips
  3. Take out a bowl, a whisk, a baking sheet, and a spoon
  4. Melt the butter
  5. Add sugar to the melted butter
  6. Mix in one egg and the vanilla extract
  7. Add flour, baking soda, and salt to the mixture
  8. Once smoothly mixed, add in chocolate chips
  9. After the chocolate chips have been evenly folded in, take a spoon scoop out balls of cookie dough, and place them on the baking sheet
  10. Bake the cookies for 7-10 minutes

Task 2: Pseudocode a simple computer game

  1. Print the images of rocks,paper, and scissors
  2. Accept user input
  3. Have the computer randomly pick between rock, paper, or scissors
  4. Compare user input and computer choice
  5. Choose winner
  6. If it is a tie, the game restarts
